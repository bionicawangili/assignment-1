Diggers Beach Volleyball

Mission

The purpose of creating website for Diggers Beach Volleyball is to improve the business by letting people know more about Volleyball and in result will attract more people to play at Diggers.

The Website contains 4 menu which is About Volleyball, Contact, Ladder and Sign Up.

Homepage: contain announcements which is very useful for both business owner and customer. Customer will know more about the promotion that is offered.

About Volleyball: This page is very useful for beginners who are interested in volleyball. Thus, Diggers will attract more new customers .

Contact: This is important page for customers if they want to keep in contact with Diggers. Enquiry form is provided for customers who wish to give feedback

Sign up: Sign up page make it convenient for customers who wants to register their team.

Success Evaluation

The evaluation process would include how long users visit the website, what pages were visited in what order, how many times a page was used from most used pages and least used pages, etc. Counters, weblogs, and capture of demographic information with cookies provide constant monitoring.

Target Audience

The client wish the website can attract younger audience therefore, the target audience for this site is high school kids to young adults

Younger people would be more attracted to colorful design so the website should be colorful and contain textured background to make it look attractive for the audience.

For the font choice I chose Helvetica which is San-Serif type of font is more suitable for sports because it is straight edged and heavier font.
